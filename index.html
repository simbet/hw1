Food & Drinks

I know what's on your mind: German haute cuisine is centered around Sausages, Sauerkraut and Beer - preferably served in a Stein.

Yes, we do love those but every once in a while we spice up our F&B intake with alternative breakfast, lunch or dinner options 😉.

Tell me fellow foodies, have you heard of "Sonntagsfrühstück", "Rostbraten mit Spätzle & Soß" or "Schweinshaxe mit Breznknödel & Dunkelbiersoß"? Too meaty? Fear not my vegetarian friends - I got you covered with "Schupfnudeln mit Kirschtomaten, Spinat und Sahnesoße" and for dessert "Dampfnudel mit Vanillesoß".

Whichever of those dishes I can get my hands on, the verdict is always the same: "Da könnt I mi neilege", i.e. "this is so good, I could fill my bathtub with it and just lay in it".
